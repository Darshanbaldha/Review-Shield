About Our Project

AI-Powered Fake Review Detection System

Project Overview

Our advanced artificial intelligence system helps identify fake reviews on e-commerce platforms to ensure authentic customer feedback and enable informed purchasing decisions. We analyze review patterns, language usage, and behavioral indicators to distinguish between genuine and fraudulent reviews.

The Problem

Fake reviews cost consumers billions of dollars annually and undermine trust in online marketplaces. Traditional detection methods are often insufficient against sophisticated fake review schemes.

Key Features

Advanced capabilities for comprehensive review analysis

Extract and analyze reviews directly from product URLs across multiple platforms

AI-Powered Detection
